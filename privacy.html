Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

Overview

Atlas Software provides Zillow Elevation Tool, a Chrome extension that adds approximate elevation badges and optional Pro auto-hide controls to Zillow listing pages the user chooses to view.

Data Used

The extension may use Zillow listing page content visible in the browser, including listing URL, visible address text, visible price text, and coordinates already present in page or map data.

The extension may send coordinates to public elevation providers to retrieve an elevation estimate. It stores extension settings, local elevation cache entries, daily lookup counters, and local entitlement state in Chrome extension storage.

Local entitlement state may include payment-provider account or subscription identifiers, such as the email address returned by ExtensionPay for license verification.

Payments

Credit-card payments are processed by Stripe through ExtensionPay. The extension does not collect or store credit-card numbers.

Data Sharing

Coordinates may be sent to elevation providers to return an elevation estimate. Payment account and subscription data is handled by ExtensionPay and Stripe for checkout, subscription management, and license verification.

Data Not Sold

The extension does not sell user data, does not run ads, and does not export Zillow listing data.

Limited Use

User data is used only to provide and improve the extension's stated features, support payment and subscription status, maintain security, and comply with legal obligations.

User Controls

Users can disable the extension on Zillow, clear the local elevation cache, and manage or cancel Pro subscriptions through the Pro management link.
