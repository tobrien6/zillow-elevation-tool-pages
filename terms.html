Zillow Elevation Tool

Terms and Refund Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

Use

Zillow Elevation Tool provides approximate elevation badges and optional auto-hide controls for visible Zillow listings. Elevation values are estimates and should not be used as survey, engineering, safety, legal, or insurance determinations.

Pro Subscription

Pro subscriptions unlock auto-hide controls, map elevation labels, and a higher daily lookup limit. Current prices are $6.99 per month or $49.99 per year. Payments are processed by Stripe through ExtensionPay.

Cancellation

Users may manage or cancel Pro subscriptions through the extension's Pro management link. Canceling prevents future renewal but does not automatically refund prior charges.

Refunds

If Pro does not work as expected, request a refund through the Chrome Web Store support link for this extension. Refunds are reviewed case by case.

Availability

The extension depends on Zillow page structure, public elevation data, browser APIs, and third-party payment services. Features may change or stop working if those dependencies change.

Affiliation

Zillow Elevation Tool is independent and is not affiliated with Zillow Group.
