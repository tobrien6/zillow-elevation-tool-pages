Terms and Refund Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

Use

Atlas Software provides Zillow Elevation Tool, a Chrome extension with approximate elevation badges and optional auto-hide controls for visible Zillow listings. Elevation values are estimates and should not be used as survey, engineering, safety, legal, insurance, or flood-risk determinations.

Pro Subscription

Pro subscriptions unlock auto-hide controls and a higher daily lookup limit. Current prices are $6.99 per month or $49.99 per year. Payments are processed by Stripe through ExtensionPay.

Cancellation

Users may manage or cancel Pro subscriptions through the extension's Pro management link. Canceling prevents future renewal but does not automatically refund prior charges.

Refunds and Disputes

If Pro does not work as expected, email tobrien6@gmail.com with the payment email and issue details. Refunds are reviewed case by case. Customers should contact support before filing a payment dispute so the issue can be reviewed.

Returns and Shipping

Zillow Elevation Tool is digital software. No physical goods are sold, shipped, or returned.

Availability
